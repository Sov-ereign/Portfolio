F-22 Raptor Model

A custom-built aircraft inspired by the F-22 Raptor, designed for the IIT KGP Laws of Motion event.

Essential raw components: D3536 1450KV Brushless Motor, 9x4.7 inch propeller, 40A ESC with BEC, and a 2200mAh LiPo battery for extended flight time.

Constructed using lightweight Fliteboard Pro, Depron sheets, and Delta-wing design inspired by the F-22 Raptor for enhanced aerodynamics.

Wingspan of 25.5 inches (0.65 meters) optimized for stability.

RadioLink and FlySky CT6B transmitter for precise control.

Advanced servo-controlled mechanisms for smooth operations.

Optimized for high aerodynamic efficiency and controlled flight maneuvers.

Capable of stable takeoff and flight durations exceeding 30 seconds. Elevons used for pitch and roll control, operated by TowerPro SG90 9g servos.

Payload system featuring a servo-controlled bay for golf ball drops.

Designed for smooth landings with minimized impact force.
